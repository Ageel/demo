How To Make A Sandwich

  1. Gather up your items: bread, mayo, sliced cheese, lettuce, sliced tomatoes and sandwich meat
  2. Wash your hands.
  3. Take two slices of bread and spread your mayo on one side.
  4. Place the sliced cheese on top of the bread with the mayo.
  5. Place your sandwich meat on top of the cheese.
  6. Place your lettuce and tomatoes on top of the sandwich meat.
  7. Place your second piece of bread on top of the vegetables, and voila, your sandwich is ready!
